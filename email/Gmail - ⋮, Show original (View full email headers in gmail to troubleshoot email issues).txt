
Viewing separate headers + body in Gmail

------------------------------------------------------------

In-Short:
	> Open Email, Click ⋮, Click Show original

------------------------------------------------------------

Step-by-Step:
	> Log into GMail using your Browser of choice
		> Open email which you wish to view the headers of
			> Select the ⋮ (vertical ellipsis) menu on the top-right
				> Select "Show original" from dropdown menu to view the original, low-level headers and body content
