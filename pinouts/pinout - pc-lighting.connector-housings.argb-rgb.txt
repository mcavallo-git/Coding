------------------------------------------------------------
ARGB / RGB Pinouts (PC Motherboard Wiring/Connector Housing Termination Standards)
------------------------------------------------------------

Corsair 

Connector Housing (3-pin / 4-pin)
  |
  |--> Dupont MX2.54 (male/female) - ARGB (5V)
  |
  |--> Molex 50-57-9404 (male), Molex 70107-0003 (female) - ARGB (5V)


------------------------------------------------------------

Phanteks

Connector Housing (3-pin / 4-pin)
  |
  |--> JST SM (2.5mm pitch) (male/female)
  |     |
  |     |--> Also sometimes referred to as an "LC4 Locking Connector"
  |
  |--> Phanteks DRGB3P (5V, 3-pin)
  |--> Phanteks RGB4P (12V, 4-pin)
