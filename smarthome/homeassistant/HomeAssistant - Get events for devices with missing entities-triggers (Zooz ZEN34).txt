GET INFO FROM DEVICES WHICH DON'T SHOW UP AS A DEVICE/ENTITY IN THE HOMEASSISTANT PORTAL
------------------------------------------------------------

In the HomeAssistant Portal/GUI:
 |
 |--> Click "Developer Tools" > "Events"  ( http://homeassistant:8123/developer-tools/event )
 |
 |--> Enter "zwave_js_value_notification" in the "Event to subscribe to" field > Click "Start Listening"
 |
 |--> Trigger the switch/event on the device which is not showing up as a device or entity
       |
       |--> Copy-down the JSON info returned by the listener
