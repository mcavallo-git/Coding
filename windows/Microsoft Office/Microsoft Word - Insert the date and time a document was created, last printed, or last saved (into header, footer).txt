Microsoft Word - Insert the date and time a document was created, last printed, or last saved (into header, footer)
  |--> Refer to citation, below

------------------------------------------------------------

Microsoft Word
 > click "Insert" (tab, top)
 	> click "Quick Parts" (dropdown under "Text" group) > click "Field"
		> click "(all)" under "Categories" > click "Date and Time" > click "SaveDate"
		> set "Date format" to "dd-MMM-yyyy HH:mm:ss"
			> click OK
